Evaluation & Commitment

To ensure a high standard of skill and commitment, we have a clear and strict evaluation policy. Success in this program is based on active participation and timely completion of tasks.

Attendance Calculation

Weekly attendance is weighted equally between live session participation and assignment submission.

Minimum Requirement

A cumulative attendance of below 75\% by **January 15th, 2026** will result in removal from the program. This policy is strictly enforced to maintain the cohort's quality and pace.

Performance Leaderboard

A points-based leaderboard will track performance. Your rank on **January 15th, 2026** determines your role and weightage in the final mini-projects, rewarding consistent effort and excellence.

Session Structure

Live sessions occur every 4 days. Each session is followed by a mandatory assignment with a 1-week deadline, ensuring a steady learning rhythm.
