15-Session Training Schedule (Dec 2025 - Jan 2026)

This section details the 15 training sessions over the 62-day Training Phase (Phase I). Sessions occur every 4 days, followed by a mandatory 1-week assignment. Coordinators lead 2-3 sessions each.

Full Program Timeline (Nov 2025 - Mar 2026)

The program is structured into four sequential phases, from mandatory setup to final project execution.

Evaluation & Accountability

Performance is measured across four criteria, culminating in a critical restructuring checkpoint at the end of the Training Phase.

Leaderboard Weighting

The final ranking is determined by a four-part score, emphasizing both commitment and technical proficiency.

Team Restructuring Policy

Following the Training Phase (Jan 31, 2026), a team restructuring will occur. Individuals who persistently miss tasks and assignments may be let go, affecting up to 33\% of the team.

Project Role Determination

The Leaderboard ranking as of February 28, 2026 (end of Workshop Phase), will determine the weightage, roles, and responsibilities DCs receive during the subsequent Mini-Project Phase (March 2026).

Session Structure

Live sessions are conducted every 4 days. Each session is immediately followed by a mandatory task and assignment with a strict 1-week deadline.
